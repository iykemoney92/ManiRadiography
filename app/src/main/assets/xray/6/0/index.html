Hip(AP)

Centering point:The vertical central ray is directed 2.5 cm distally along the perpendicular bisector of a line joining the anterior superior iliac spine and the symphysis pubis over the femoral pulse

Cassette Size:24cm x 30cm (12 x 10 ins) Potrait

Exposure Factors:70kVp on 20MaS

FFD:100cm

Bucky/Grid:Moving or Stationary Grid

Filter:No

Collimation:Collimate to four sides of interest

Pathologies:Fracture, Lesions, Joint narrowing

Position of patient and cassette

  • The patient lies supine and symmetrical on the X-ray table, with the median sagittal plane perpendicular to the tabletop
  • To avoid pelvic rotation, the anterior superior iliac spines must be equidistant from the tabletop
  • The affected limb is internally rotated to bring the neck of the femur parallel to the tabletop, and is then supported by sandbags
