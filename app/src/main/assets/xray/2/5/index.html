Nasal Bone(Lateral)

Centering point:A horizontal central ray is directed through the centre of the nasal bones and collimated to include the nose.

Cassette Size:24cm x 30cm (12 x 10 ins) Landscape

Exposure Factors:66kVp on 15mAs

FFD:100cm

Bucky/Grid:Moving or Stationary Grid

Filter:No

Collimation:Collimate to area of interest

Pathologies:Nasal foreign bodies, Nasal Fractures, Nasal injuries

Position of patient and cassette

  • The patient sits facing an 18  24-cm cassette supported in the cassette stand of a vertical Bucky.
  • The head is turned so that the median sagittal plane is parallel with the cassette and the inter-pupillary line is perpendicular to the cassette.
  • The nose should be roughly coincident with the centre of the cassette.
