Skull(Submento vertical view)

Centering point:Right-angles to the orbito-meatal plane and centred midway between the external auditory meatuses

Cassette Size:24 x 30cm (10 x 12ins) Portrait

Exposure Factors:70kVp on 20MaS

FFD:100cm

Pathologies:#(Fracture), Trauma, FB(Foreign body),Idiopathic

Position of patient and cassette

  • The patient may be imaged erect or supine. If the patient is unsteady, then a supine technique is advisable.
  • The patient’s shoulders are raised and the neck is hyperextended to bring the vertex of the skull in contact with the grid cassette or table.
    • .
  • The head is adjusted to bring the external auditory meatuses equidistant from the cassette.
  • The median sagittal plane should be at right-angles to the cassette along its midline.
  • The orbito-meatal plane should be as near as possible parallel to the cassette
