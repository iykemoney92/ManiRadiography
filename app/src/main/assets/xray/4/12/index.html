Humerus(Lateral)

Centering point:Central ray to midpoint of humerus and perpendicular

Cassette Size:43 x 35cm (17 x 14ins) Potrait

Exposure Factors:60kVp on 15MaS

FFD:100cm

Bucky/Grid:No

Filter:No

Pathologies:Fractures, bone lesions, foreign bodies

Position of patient and cassette

  • Position patient erect with their back against the upright bucky and elbow partially flexed.
  • Body slightly rotated towards the affected side to bring humerus and shoulder in contact with cassette.
  • Internally rotate arm to achieve a lateral position (epicondyles perpendicular to cassette).
  • Central ray to midpoint of humerus and perpendicular to cassette.
