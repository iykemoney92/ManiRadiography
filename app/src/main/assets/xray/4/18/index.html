Sternoclavicular Joint(Lateral)

Centering point:The horizontal central ray is centred to the palpable sternoclavicular joints just below the sternal notch.

Cassette Size:18 x 24cm (8 x 10ins)Landscape

Exposure Factors:66kVp on 20MaS

FFD:100cm

Bucky/Grid:Moving or Stationary Grid

Filter:No

Pathologies:Fracture

Position of patient and cassette

  • The patient sits or stands sideways with the affected side adjacent to the cassette.
  • The median sagittal plane is adjusted parallel to the cassette with the upper arm in contact with it.
  • the patient clasps the hands behind and pulls the shoulders well back to avoid any obscuring of the joints.
  • The centre of the cassette is adjusted to coincide with the level of the sternoclavicular joints.
