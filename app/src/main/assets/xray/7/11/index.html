Tibia & Fibula(Lateral)

Centering point:Centre to the middle of the cassette, with the central ray at right-angles to the long axis of the tibia and parallel to an imaginary line joining the malleoli

Cassette Size:43cm x 35cm (17 x 14 ins) Potrait

Exposure Factors:60kVp on 10MaS

FFD:100cm

Bucky/Grid:No

Filter:No

Pathologies:Fractures, lesions

Position of patient and cassette

  • From the supine/seated position, the patient rotates on to the affected side.
  • The leg is rotated further until the malleoli are superimposed vertically.
  • The tibia should be parallel to the cassette.
  • The tibia should be parallel to the cassette.
  • The lower edge of the cassette is positioned just below the plantar aspect of the heel.
